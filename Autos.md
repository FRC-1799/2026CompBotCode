## Outlines the Current Autos 
Autos can be selected via the sendable chooser published to network tables at "/SmartDashboard/auto chooser". Note that this chooser is only used at the start of the autonomous period and so changes will not take effect until the robot is reenabled in autonomous mode. 


Spin: Spins the robot around and does nothing else
Depo: Makes a sweep through mid to intake, Shoots, Grabs pieces from the Depo and shoots again. 
Outpost: Makes a sweep through mid to intake, Shoots, Grabs pieces from the Outpost and shoots again. 
DoubleMid: Makes two sweeps through mid shooting after each one.
